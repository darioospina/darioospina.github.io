Who's Egan Bernal?

Egan Arley Bernal Gómez is a Colombian cyclist who won the 2019 Tour of France, becoming the first Latin American winning this race.

Egan was born in Bogota in 1997, however he was raised in Zipaquira, a small town nearby.

Professional career

Egan started at a very young age competing in mountain biking lower categories, winning races in Brazil, Costa Rica and the United States. By 2015 he had won bronze and silver medals in world championships.

In 2016, Bernal was signed by Gianni Savio to the Androni Giocattoli–Sidermec team on a four year contract. In 2018 he made his debut for Team Sky in the Tour Down Under as team co-leader

Besides the Tour of France, Bernal has won gold medals in important competitions such as Tour of Bihor in 2016, Sibiu Cycling Tour, Tour de Savoie Mont Blanc and Tour de l'Avenir in 2017, Tour of California in 2018 and Paris–Nice and Tour de Suisse, both in 2019, the same year he won the Tour of France.
